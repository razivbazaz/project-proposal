Introduction

Plus Promotions will design and develop the RemoteStaff.ie website to clearly communicate your unique selling points and strengthen credibility with Irish business audiences. The following document defines page structure, design principles, core functionalities, and deliverables within the Plus Promotions visual identity.

Project Objectives

  • Launch a modern, SEO-optimised website for RemoteStaff.ie.
  • Highlight eight key USPs prominently across pages and CTAs.
  • Integrate an interactive Cost-Saving Calculator to compare Irish vs. South African staffing costs.
  • Create structured “Hire Talent” landing pages grouped by profession.

Website Scope Overview

  • Seven primary pages: Home, Why Choose Us, Hire Talent, How It Works, Pricing (Savings Calculator), About, and Contact.
  • Fifteen profession-specific subpages under Hire Talent.

How It Works

  1. Share Your Requirement – define skills, schedule, and experience.
  2. Shortlisting – candidates lined up within 48–72 hours.
  3. Interview – conduct preferred interview rounds and shortlist.
  4. Hire & Contract – final selection, contract setup, and onboarding within a week.

Fast & Simple Hiring

Pricing – “See How Much You Save”

Interactive calculator comparing Irish local hire vs. RemoteStaff placement.

Inputs

  • Select Role (prefilled with Irish market salary data).
  • Irish Annual Salary (editable).
  • Employer PRSI % (default 11.05%), Pension % (3–5% from Jan 2026), Overheads €/month (default €350).
  • RemoteStaff Monthly Fee (pre-set by role).

Outputs

  • Table: Local Hire vs. RemoteStaff total annual cost.
  • Estimated savings (€ + %), bar graph or chart visual.
  • CTA: “Get Shortlist of Candidates”.
