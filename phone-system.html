Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about business phone systems, Cloud PBX and VoIP for SMEs in Ireland.

Can we keep our existing business number?

In many cases, yes. Number porting is common when moving to a cloud phone system. We’ll confirm feasibility during setup.

How long does installation take?

It depends on user count and call flow complexity. Many SME deployments can be planned and deployed quickly once requirements are confirmed.

Do we need to buy new IP phones?

Not always. If your current devices are compatible, they may be reused. If not, we can supply pre-configured IP phones and handsets.

Will it work for remote employees?

Yes. Users can answer calls using desk phones, mobile apps or softphones, keeping a consistent business number across devices.
