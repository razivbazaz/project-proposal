Pulse Voice & Data — Website Redevelopment & SEO Strategy

Rebuild Pulse.ie with an SEO‑first structure and an integrated Shop for telecom hardware. Position Pulse as Ireland’s one‑stop provider for business phone systems, Hosted PBX, VoIP for SMEs, and unified communications while enabling direct product sales.

Why now

Modern UX + measurable SEO growth

Elevate brand perception, increase organic traffic, and convert visitors with clear pricing, comparison blocks, and fast checkout.

Key outcome

Service-to-Product ecosystem

Every service page connects to compatible hardware (IP phones, PBX units, headsets), creating a seamless discovery → purchase journey.

Positioning

Establish Pulse.ie as a top‑of‑search B2B telecom brand in Ireland with authoritative service pages and category‑led product listings.

Generate qualified leads for hosted telephony and drive direct revenue via a streamlined eCommerce layer for telecom hardware.

  • Resources / Blog
  • Contact / Get a Quote

UX Highlights

  • Dual funnel on Home: “Get a Quote” and “Shop Hardware”
  • Product cards with badges (SIP‑ready, Teams‑compatible, PoE, Warranty)
  • Comparison tables & downloadable spec sheets
  • Cross‑links: Service → Compatible Products → Add to Cart
  • Schema markup for Service and Product entities
  • Fast, accessible, mobile‑first layouts (Core Web Vitals ready)
